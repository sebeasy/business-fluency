Engish Grammar at B1

In English we can use either will ('ll) to talk about the future in a formal situation. Eg: I will collect Mr Davis at the airport. Also at the moment you decide about the future, including an immediate future. Eg: I'll help you with those heavy shopping bags.

When we have already decided about the future, we have made plans. We use use be going to. Eg: I'm going to the station in 10 minutes. Do you want a ride?
